Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Tonkinese cat?

Tonkinese cats are a medium-sized breed with a muscular build and a silky coat. They are known for their affectionate and social nature.

How do I adopt a Tonkinese kitten?

You can adopt a Tonkinese kitten by contacting our cattery through the contact form on our website. We will guide you through the adoption process.

What is the temperament of a Tonkinese cat?

Tonkinese cats are known for their friendly and playful nature. They enjoy interacting with their human companions and are often described as being dog-like in their loyalty and affection.

How do I care for a Tonkinese kitten?

Caring for a Tonkinese kitten involves regular feeding with high-quality cat food, ensuring they have plenty of playtime and social interaction, and regular veterinary check-ups to keep them healthy.

When will the kittens be ready to go home after they are born?

We keep the kittens until they are 12 weeks old, then they can go to their new homes.

What is the cost of a Tonkinese kitten?

To be fair for everyone, we collect a non-refundable $300 deposit to add you to the waitlist of a litter. The date we receive your reservation fee will determine your place on our reservation list. For instance, first fee gets first pick, second fee received gets second pick, regardless of male or female preference. The price is determined by confirmation and temperament of the kitten.

What certifications and credentials does your cattery have?

Our cattery is fully registered with The International Cat Association (TICA) and The World Cat Federation (WCF). We have over 15 years of breeding experience and maintain the highest standards for health and temperament.
