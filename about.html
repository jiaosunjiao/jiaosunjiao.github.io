Our Story

We are a small cattery in Tucson, Arizona. We have been breeding and showing cats for 15 years. We are both retired and our cats and kittens live with us underfoot. Our priorities are health, temperament and conformation.

We began showing cats in 2009. We have had Persians and American Shorthairs win Lifetime Achievement, International and Regional awards.

We fell in love with the Tonkinese breed at a cat show and have now put our focus and emphasis on this lovely breed.

Once you own one of our kittens, we are here for you to help and answer any and all questions. You become part of our Tonkinese family.
