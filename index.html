Welcome to Carolyn & Sarah's Tonkinese

Your source for adorable Tonkinese. We are a small, closed two-people run cattery located in beautiful Arizona.

At Carolyn and Sarah's Tonkinese, we treat our kittens as our children and they grow up in our living room, bedroom, and our arms.

We are dedicated to breeding and raising healthy, happy kittens that make perfect companions. Our cattery is committed to ensuring the best care for our kittens and providing them with loving homes. All our cats and kitten litters are registered with The International Cat Association (TICA) or The Cat Fanciers Association (CFA).
